###### **#The Original 3 Ideas**



The idea of making a game series called "Elements" and a lot of the supporting ideas came from one walk a while ago, let me walk you through it.



How i got the ideas isn't important, but the fact is i got it while I was walking. I came up with 3 game ideas at the same time:

* A game where every level added a "layer". A layer could be anything like a surface, terrain, obstacles and forces like gravity
* A game where the essence was all about enjoying the environment and understanding how every action and all motion has consequences
* A game purely about survival, where in a contained 3d world, you have to pick a particular spot before a countdown finishes. Once the countdown is over the game skips a certain amount of years (eg 1,000 years) and if over the years the player gets trapped or killed the game is lost.



I came up with names for each of the games as well, respectively:

* Layers
* Motion
* Preservation



And i thought of joining the game ideas under a game series called Elements.
